## OUTREACH STRATEGY NOTES

### Buenos Aires Restaurants
- **Best approach**: Instagram DM + in-person visit during off-peak hours
- **Language**: Spanish (mandatory)
- **Pitch angle**: "Your guests are standing on the sidewalk for 45 minutes. What if they could walk around Palermo and get a WhatsApp message when their table is ready?"
- **Email pattern**: Most BA restaurants use Gmail or personal email, not corporate domains. Go through Instagram

### LATAM Chains
- **Best approach**: LinkedIn (COO, VP Operations, CTO) + formal email
- **Decision makers**: COO or VP of Operations (not CEO for chains)
- **Pitch angle**: "Reduce walkaway rate by 30%. Your 200+ locations lose X guests per day who see the line and leave"
- **Email patterns**: firstname.lastname@company.com.br / .com.mx / .com.co

### Global Chains
- **Best approach**: LinkedIn + conference networking (NRA Show, MAPIC, etc.)
- **Decision makers**: Chief Digital Officer, VP Technology, Head of Customer Experience
- **Pitch angle**: "WhatsApp has 80%+ penetration in your markets. Your customers already use it. Meet them where they are"

### Non-Restaurant
- **Best approach**: RFP processes for government, direct outreach for private
- **Decision makers**: COO, Head of Operations, CIO
- **Pitch angle**: "Queue management = customer satisfaction = NPS improvement"
